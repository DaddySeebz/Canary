Salesforce Revenue Validation Rules for Canary
=================================================

1. Opportunity IDs must be unique in the opportunity export.
2. Amount_usd must be populated on every opportunity row.
3. Contract_end_date must be after contract_start_date in the opportunity export.
4. Every booking must match an opportunity by opportunity_id, and booked_arr_usd should reconcile to amount_usd within 1.00 USD.
5. Every revenue schedule row must match a booking by booking_id.
